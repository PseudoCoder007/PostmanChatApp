## PRODUCT OVERVIEW — WHAT THE APP DOES

Think of Postman Quest Chat as **WhatsApp + Discord + Duolingo, but built for emotional connection and personal growth.**

Here is what every user experiences when they open the app:

### Real-Time Group Chat
Create a room for any topic — a study group, a gaming clan, a friend circle, a club. Messages appear instantly for everyone in the room. You can attach images, videos, and documents. You can see who is typing. The room stays alive even when you close the app.

### Direct Messages (Friend-Gated)
You can only DM someone after you both accept a friend request. This keeps conversations safe and meaningful — no spam, no strangers sliding into your inbox. Every DM shows you whether your message was delivered (✓) or actually read (✓✓), just like WhatsApp.

### XP, Coins & Leaderboard
Every message you send, every quest you complete, every day you stay active earns you XP (experience points) and coins. Your XP determines your level and rank on the global leaderboard. Coins unlock real features inside the app — this is not cosmetic decoration, it is a real economy that rewards engagement.

### Life Quests
The app gives you quests — small, meaningful challenges like "Send an encouraging message to a friend today" or "Share something you learned this week." Some are daily habits. Some are social. Some are generated just for you by Igris, your AI companion. Completing quests earns coins and pushes you toward healthier, happier habits.

### Igris — Your AI Companion
Igris is a digital companion powered by Meta's LLaMA AI. It lives inside the app and is there whenever you need it — for a conversation, for advice, for a quest suggestion, or just to vent. Igris speaks Gen Z. It is not a corporate chatbot.

### Smart Notifications
The app notifies you when someone messages you, @mentions you, or sends a friend request — inside the app, as a browser notification, or by email. You stay connected even when the app is closed.

### v2.1.0 — Chat UX & Safety Update

**Organised Your Conversations**

Before this update, all chats were in one list. Now the app has two clear tabs:
- **DMs tab** — all your one-on-one conversations with friends
- **Groups tab** — all the rooms you have joined

Each tab shows a badge with the number of unread messages so you never miss anything.

---

**Quick Actions from Every Conversation**

Inside any DM, there is now a small settings menu (three dots ⋮) that lets you:
- **View Profile** — see your friend's avatar, level, XP, coins, and whether they are online right now, without leaving the chat
- **Unfriend** — remove them from your friends list if needed
- **Block** — stop them from messaging you entirely

This means you never have to navigate away to manage your relationships.

---

**Read Receipts — Know When Your Message Was Seen**

Every message you send in a DM now shows a small tick mark:
- **✓ (one tick)** — your message was delivered to the server
- **✓✓ (two ticks)** — your friend has opened the conversation and read it

Exactly like WhatsApp. No more wondering if someone is ignoring you or just hasn't seen it yet.

---

**Block & Unblock — A Safer Social Space**

If someone makes you uncomfortable, you can block them with one tap. Once blocked:
- They cannot send you messages
- They disappear from your search results
- They cannot see you in search either

You can unblock them anytime from the People tab. Safety is built in, not bolted on.

---

**Peer Profile Modal — See Who You Are Talking To**

When you tap "View Profile" in a DM, a clean profile card appears showing:
- The person's profile photo
- Their current level and XP
- Their coin balance
- Whether they are online right now

This makes the social identity layer feel real — you are talking to a person with a history and a rank, not just a username.

---

**Navbar Search — Find Anything Instantly**

Type anything into the top search bar and the app jumps directly to the right section. Search for "quests" and you land on the Quests tab. Search for a friend's name and their profile appears. This makes the app feel fast and intuitive even for first-time users.

### v2.2.0 — Chat Engagement & Collaboration Update

This is our biggest feature release yet — six new features that make conversations richer, more organised, and more expressive.

---

**Message Reactions — Express Yourself Without Typing**

Hover over any message and six emoji reactions appear: 👍 ❤️ 😂 😮 😢 🔥

- Tap one to react. Tap it again to remove your reaction.
- Everyone in the room sees reactions update in real time — no page refresh needed.
- You can see exactly who reacted with what emoji.

This is the single most-requested feature in social apps. A quick reaction keeps a conversation alive even when you have nothing to say.

---

**@Mentions — Tag Someone When It Matters**

When you need a specific person's attention in a busy group room, type @ and their username. A list of members appears and you can select the right person.

- The mentioned person gets a notification immediately.
- **Critically: @mention notifications arrive even if the person has muted the room.** You cannot miss an urgent ping.
- Mentions are highlighted in the message so they stand out visually.

This replaces the chaos of "hey [name]" buried in a scrolling group chat.

---

**Pinned Messages — Keep What Matters at the Top**

Room admins and owners can pin up to 5 messages to the top of any room. A collapsible banner appears at the top of the chat showing the pinned content.

**Why this matters:** In a study group, pin the exam date. In a team room, pin the decision that was made. In a club, pin the event details. Important information no longer gets buried under hundreds of messages.

Tapping the banner opens a modal showing all pinned messages at once.

---

**Room Mute — Focus Without Missing What Matters**

Every room now has a mute toggle. When you mute a room:
- Regular message notifications are silenced
- **@mentions still come through** — you will always be alerted when someone specifically needs you

This is perfect for joining a large community room you want to be part of without being interrupted every few minutes. You read at your own pace, but you never miss a direct call for your attention.

---

**Draft Persistence — Never Lose a Half-Written Message**

When you are typing a long message in one room and need to switch to another conversation, the app automatically saves your draft. When you come back to that room, your unfinished message is exactly where you left it.

A small "Draft" badge appears on the room in your sidebar so you remember you were mid-thought. Once you send the message, the draft clears. This sounds small — it is surprisingly important for anyone who juggles multiple conversations.

---

**Message Search — Find Any Message Instantly**

Every room now has a search bar. Type at least 2 characters and the app searches through the room's message history and shows up to 50 matching results.

- Search is case-insensitive — typing "meeting" finds "Meeting" and "MEETING"
- Results are fast — results are cached for 30 seconds so repeated searches feel instant
- Search is scoped to the current room — you only see results relevant to where you are

This replaces the frustrating habit of scrolling up through hundreds of messages to find something from last week.

### Use Case 1 — The College Study Squad

**The scenario:** Four engineering students are preparing for finals. They are using WhatsApp for coordination but it is chaos — important messages get buried, everyone gets notified for every message, and there is no way to highlight what matters.

**How Postman Quest Chat solves it:**

They create a **private group room** for their study group. Only they can join.

- The professor's exam schedule and the key formula sheet get **pinned at the top of the room** — visible to everyone, always, without scrolling.
- During the week before exams when everyone is stressed and busy, each person **mutes the room** so they are not constantly interrupted.
- When someone has an urgent question at midnight, they type **@username** to alert that specific person. The mention notification arrives even though the room is muted.
- When someone shares a useful resource, the others react with **👍** so the sender knows it was seen without needing a "thanks!" reply from each person.
- **Message search** means finding "the formula we discussed on Tuesday" takes 3 seconds instead of 5 minutes of scrolling.
- Completing quests together earns them coins and XP — the group leaderboard makes study sessions unexpectedly fun.

**What WhatsApp/Telegram cannot do:** WhatsApp has no pinned message limit control, no XP, no quest system, no search that is this fast, and no @mention that bypasses mute.

## TECHNOLOGY & ARCHITECTURE

Postman Quest Chat is not a prototype. It is a production-grade, containerised, deployed application with CI/CD, database migrations, secured APIs, and a real HTTPS domain. The engineering is production-ready today.

### Tech Stack (Plain English)

| Layer | What It Means |
|-------|--------------|
| **Frontend — React 18** | The app users see and click on. Fast, smooth, works on any modern browser |
| **Backend — Spring Boot 3** | The engine powering all the logic: messages, quests, friends, XP, coins |
| **Auth — Supabase** | Login and signup handled by a trusted, battle-tested auth platform |
| **Database — Supabase Postgres** | All data stored securely in a managed database with automatic backups |
| **File Storage — Supabase Storage** | Images, videos, and documents uploaded by users stored safely in the cloud |
| **AI — Meta LLaMA 3.1-70B** | The AI model powering Igris — one of the world's most capable open-source AI models |
| **Hosting — AWS EC2 + HTTPS** | The app lives on Amazon's cloud infrastructure, secured with an SSL certificate |
| **Deploy — Docker + GitHub Actions** | Every code update is automatically tested and deployed; zero manual work |

### Why the Tech Matters for Investors

- **Live today** — not a demo, not a prototype. Real users can sign up right now at postmanchat.live.
- **Horizontally scalable** — the architecture is designed so we can add more servers as users grow, with no engineering rework.
- **Secure by design** — every API call is authenticated; WebSocket connections require a one-time backend-issued ticket; no anonymous access.
- **Database migration system** — schema changes are tracked and applied safely, meaning the database can evolve as the product grows without downtime.
